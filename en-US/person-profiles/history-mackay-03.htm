Mackay came to Taiwan and encountered difficulties

【Foreword】

　　At the age of 26, Mackay finally came to Taiwan to preach, and he loved Taiwan, within five months of coming to Taiwan, he could preach in Taiwanese and established his first church in Tamsui. At that time, he was greatly hindered by the local residents, and later he preached through medical means, established the Hobe Mackay Hospital, helped the people to extract teeth and treat malaria, and successfully launched the missionary journey in Northern Taiwan, establishing a total of 60 churches.

　　Mackay's missionary secret is to travel continuously, traveling throughout half of Taiwan, and after a short stay in Tamsui, he began a month-long Northern expedition to Puli and Sun Moon Lake, and then climbed the Snowy Mountains twice in a year, as deep as the alpine aboriginal forbidden lands with the spirit of an adventurer. He crossed Sanshaoling more than 20 times in and out of Yilan, deep into the Qingpu villages in the South, and even as far as Hualien several times; he also sailed to the three Northern islands such as Pengjiayu, Cotton Island, and Huaping Islet, and even established churches on Guishan Island, which were rarely set footed by foreign missionaries. When he led the students to Southern Taiwan for a meeting, he also chose to travel back and forth on foot.

　　After settling in Tamsui, Mackay actively studied Taiwanese and Chinese, gave full play to his ability to speak well, spread Christian doctrine with concepts and sayings that were easy for the Chinese to understand, and then gradually overcame differences and rapidly expanded the believer class. In addition to his missionary career, Mackay took in a group of believers as students, taught them Western knowledge such as Roman pinyin, astronomical geography, and medical pharmacology, and led them to various parts of the North to spread the seeds of missionaries.

　　Dr. Maxwell landed in Takao (present-day Kaohsiung Harbor) and later entered the city of Tainan City to begin his missionary medical work. At that time, Maya would preach first and then see the doctor and dispense medicine, the process was very arduous, and soon after practicing medicine, it was opposed and rumored by the people. Later, after being transferred to Fengshan, the first Western Hospital in Taiwan was established and a chapel was set up, and the results of its medical preaching were remarkable.


【Solution】

　　To communicate more fluently with the residents of Tamsui to preach, he found a shepherd boy nearby, practiced talking with him every day in Hokkien, and after five months of hard training, Mackay finally stood on the podium and began to try to preach to the people of Tamsui in Hokkien.

　　To rectify the churches destroyed and occupied by the Japanese army and rescue the believers arrested for resisting Japan, Mackay exerted his outstanding action and eloquence, directly meeting with the former governors of Taiwan, Nogi Heidori and Kodama Gentaro, to state the destruction of the church and the tragic situation of the arrest and killing of the believers, and finally obtained the official promise to guarantee the freedom of mission and the safety of the believers.

　　In the early years of Japanese rule, Mackay actively assisted Japanese missionaries in establishing a church in Taiwan, and also personally preached doctrine to Japanese believers and baptized them, and the friendship and love gained from them also helped Mackay to carry out his missionary cause under Japanese rule.


【Epilogue】

　　Mackay began his missionary activities in Taiwan, where he devoted his life to the island. From Mackay’s childhood to he was about to land in Taiwan, from this highly educated and highly cultivated person, we can see the power of religious influence! The most important thing for a person who lives according to God is his worships is the persistence of faith, which shows the hidden source of a person's character and is also the true measure of a person's inner self it is also because Mackay's persistence in faith that he can stand up in constant frustration! Whenever he fails, he always reminds himself that this is because his ability is not enough, God is testing him, let me admire quite much, for what I can't do, I always use various reasons to prevaricate, evade, and not want to face it, because I am afraid of failure, afraid of trouble, afraid of shame; finally, I ran away from everything, sloppily handled the things I did not want to touch and finally ignored the countless opportunities, let it float before my eyes and disappear, and then complain about the world! Now think about it, it is you who allow others, not the opportunity you did not have! Successful trials are still obtained by their hard work and find!

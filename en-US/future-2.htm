Commemoration Actions


This year marks Mackay's 150th anniversary.
There are also many ways to commemorate Mackay in Taiwan and cultural relics.
There are many statues commemorating Mackay in Tamsui, for example, in 2001, just as the centenary of Mackay's death, the mayor of Tamsui town set up a bronze statue of Mackay in this place of Mackay Street, and also held a memorial service in front of the statue at that time. On the spot, it was also announced that June 2 of each year is Mackay Day, which is also the anniversary of Taiwan's first local government announcement, which can be said to be very meaningful, Taiwan not only set up statues but also held memorial meetings from time to time, but also made commemorative stamps that are expected to be released this year

Canada's Remembrance
In the Canadian side of the Taipei Economic and Cultural Director in Toronto, visited Oxford County Governor Larry Martin, together to discuss the commemoration of the 150th anniversary of Mackay this year, there is also a donation of a bronze statue to Canada at Aletheia University in Taiwan, not only to raise the friendship between Taiwan and Canada but also to commemorate Mackay, it is expected that this year there will be a short film commemorating Mackay and stamps, as well as a monument to Mackay.

When he first came to Taiwan, in order to integrate into the life of Taiwanese people, through talking with farmers and shepherd boys, learning the completely unfamiliar Taiwanese dialect, in the process, everyone shouted "beard aborigine" because he was a foreigner, and then everyone flew away, tried again and again, and finally he became good friends with everyone. Learning a language was not an easy task, and Mackay spent a lot of time and effort to speak the Taiwanese dialect fluently for the sake of ideals and love for Taiwan, and communicated with the Taiwanese at that time completely without obstacles.
